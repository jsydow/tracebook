TraceBook - Help

Welcome to TraceBook help. Here you get a closer look to all features of this tracking application. Just select one of the screens below to get further information. To get information about the functionality of the actual screen you are, just enable the checkbox to show the statusbar on top of each screen. With a short click at the screen name, you get a description of all possibility the screen has to afford.


If your question find no answer in this manual just feel free to ask us at the project page.
